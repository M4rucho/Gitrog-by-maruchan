Drawing the library

Requirements

  • Dakmore salvage in hand
  • A discard outlet on the battlefield
  • The Gitrog Monster on the battlefield

Procedure

  1. Discard dakmore, Gitrog tiggers
  2. Resolve the draw trigger choosing to replace it with the dakmore dredge
At this point there a few things that could happend:
  • You mill a shuffeler
  • You mill no land cards
  • You mill at least one land
If you milled at least one land hold on the trigger,resolve any other trigger you have (like a shufeler or something like that) and discard dakmore again, if you milled no land cards discard dakmore again and repeat the process until you generate enough draw triggers so you can draw all your library

Infinite mana

    Sorcery speed

    Requirements

    • No cards in your library or your graveyard
    • Gitrog on the battlefield
    • Discard outlet on the battlefield

    Procedure

    1. Cast lotus petal (In case you didn't have it already on the battlefield).
    2. Discard a land, in response to the Gitrogs trigger discard Ulamog (Or a shuffeler) and shuffle.
    3. Holding the draw trigger, discard dakmore and mill, resolve ONLY the shuffle (Now you'll have 2 draw triggers on the stack).
    4. Discard dakmore, in response to the Gitrog trigger crack lotus petal and choose to dredge.
    5. Resolve the shuffle and then draw 3 cards.

    NOW YOU WILL GENERATE INFINITE MANA!! (reapeating those steps as many times as you want)

    Instant speed (only produces black mana)

    Requirements

    • No cards in your library or your graveyard
    • Gitrog on the battlefield
    • Discard outlet on the battlefield
    • at least 1 black mana aveliable

    Procedure

  • Discard a land, in response to the Gitrogs trigger discard Ulamog (Or a shuffeler) and shuffle.
  • Holding the draw trigger, discard dakmore and mill, resolve ONLY the shuffle (Now you'll have 2 draw triggers on the stack).
  • Discard dakmore, in response to the Gitrog trigger cast dark ritual and choose to dredge.
  • Resolve the shuffle and then draw 3 cards.

Instant speed

Requirements

  • No cards in your library or your graveyard
  • Gitrog on the battlefield
  • Discard outlet on the battlefield
  • Infinite black mana

Procedure

  1. Discard a land, in response to the Gitrogs trigger discard Ulamog (Or a shuffeler) and shuffle.
  2. Holding the draw trigger, discard dakmore and mill, resolve ONLY the shuffle (Now you'll have 2 draw triggers on the stack).
  3. Repeat step 2 (Now you have 3 draw triggers on the stack)
  4. Discard dakmore, in response to the Gitrog trigger cast Orcish Bow Masters (OBM) , pin an oponent and choose to dredge.
  5. Cast dismember targeting OBM
  6. Resolve the shuffle and then draw 4 cards.
Repeat Infinite times
